Ingredients



For Milk Tea:

  • Tea Leaves or Tea Bags
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Spices (like cardamom, ginger)

For Black Tea:

  • Tea Leaves or Tea Bags
  • Water
  • Sugar or Honey
  • Spices (like Cloves, ginger)

For Green Tea:

  • Green Tea Leaves or Bags
  • Hot Water
  • Mint Leaves (optional)
  • Honey (optional)

For Lemon Tea:

  • Tea Leaves or Bags
  • Squeezed Lemon
  • Sugar or Honey
  • Water
  • Spices (like ginger, mint)

For Bubble Tea:

  • Black Tea or Green Tea (strong brewed)
  • Tapioca Pearls (boba)
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • Fruits Syrups (optional)

For Chocoate Tea:

  • Tea Leaves
  • Cocoa Shells (for chocolate flavor)
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Water
